Kolkata Knight Riders

Team Owners: Shahrukh Khan, Zuhi chawla.

The Kolkata Knight Riders a.k.a. KKR is a franchise cricket team based in Kolkata, which participates in the Indian Premier League. Kolkata Knight Riders was founded in 2008. Bollywood Superstar, Shah Rukh Khan owns the franchise along with the Bollywood Diva, Juhi Chawla. The team has Eden Gardens, in Kolkata for its home ground. The team is currently led by the wicketkeeper-batsmanDinesh Karthik. KKR has won two IPL titles so far in 2012 and 2014 respectively.
