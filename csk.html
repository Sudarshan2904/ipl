Chennai Super Kings

Team Owners: Subramaniam Palaniappan, Srinivasan Ranganathan.

Chennai Super Kings is a cricket franchise which makes its appearances in the Indian Premier League. Popularly known as CSK, the team was founded in 2008 by India Cements and has been one of the most dominant teams in the Indian Premier League.

The team is led by Mahendra Singh Dhoni and has always reached the playoffs, except in 2016 and 2017, when it was suspended. Chennai Super Kings have won 3 IPL Titles so far, and have been the Runners Up on 4 occasions.
