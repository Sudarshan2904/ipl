Mumbai Indians

Team Owners: Akash Ambani, Neeta Ambani.

Mumbai Indians is the most successful franchise in IPL history with four titles in 2013, 2015, 2017 and 2019. They have also won the Champions League t20 twice (2011 and 2013). Arguably, the team with the biggest fan base.

Mumbai Indians are currently captained by Rohit Sharma . Mahela Jayawardene was appointed as head coach of Mumbai Indians before the 2017 season.

The team logo is the Sudarshan Chakra (or Razor) as initially the name of the team was supposed to be "Mumbai Razors" before Sachin Tendulkar suggested to keep it Mumbai Indians.
